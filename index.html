ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Raka Ikbar Setyadireza, a fresh graduate in Informatics Engineering from Indonesia, class of 2024. I have a strong passion for designing and developing websites, transforming creative ideas into functional digital experiences.

Beyond web design, I enjoy working on graphic design projects, including logos, posters, and other visual content. My passion extends to game design, where I have created and developed 2D games, blending creativity with technical implementation.

I'm always eager to learn and explore new technologies to enhance my skills and create impactful digital experiences
